Our Story

Born From Passion,
Brewed For Connection.

Nestled in the heart of Pangkalan Bun, Central Kalimantan, RASYA COFFEE was born from a simple belief: that every cup of coffee should tell a story. Our founder, driven by an unwavering passion for the perfect brew, embarked on a journey to bring world-class coffee experiences to our beloved community.

From carefully sourcing the finest beans to training our baristas in the art of extraction, every detail is meticulously crafted. We do not just serve coffee, we create moments of connection, warmth, and pure indulgence.

At RASYA, you are not just a customer; you are part of our story. Come, let us brew something beautiful together.
